This project has significantly developed my technical skills, deepened my knowledge of complex circuitry, and improved my proficiency in C++. It also emphasizes the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration and real-world problem-solving, preparing me for future challenges in engineering.

DIY Antenna

Integrated Performance Task

The project aimed to simulate communication methods in natural disaster zones, requiring us to develop Python scripts to convert messages into Morse code signals. Additionally, we constructed an antenna for receiving and deciphering Morse code signals transmitted by another group. This interdisciplinary challenge tested my understanding of the Fourier Transform for signal processing and honed my programming skills in Python. Navigating this project presented several challenges, including ensuring the accuracy and reliability of our Morse code conversion scripts and optimizing antenna design for effective signal reception. Overcoming these challenges involved rigorous testing, iterative improvements, and collaborative problem-solving among team members with diverse backgrounds in engineering and programming. Through this experience, I gained a deeper appreciation for the practical applications of theoretical concepts like the Fourier Transform in real-world scenarios. It reinforced the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration and adaptability in overcoming technical challenges, preparing me for future projects that require innovative solutions at the intersection of engineering and communication technologies.

Achievement: Scholarship Winner

Participating the 2024 Al Worden Endeavour Challenge was a transformative experience. My team and I were tasked with planning a mission to Proxima Centauri, designing a spacecraft capable of long-distance travel and accommodating human needs. This involved extensive research and collaboration, enhancing our teamwork and problem-solving skills. There were challenges during the planning part of the competiton as we were not able to physically meet other, resulting in slow progres initially. However, we were able to overcome it as we coordinate and delegated task to each other, making sure everyone was responsible in finishing thier part. On competition day, the whole team extremely nervous and felt under prepared, however we were confident that we could pull it off as we had prepared for it and supported each other. Overcoming these obstacles required effective communication, quick thinking, and leveraging our collective expertise. We had to adapt rapidly to unexpected problems, demonstrating resilience and resourcefulness. The experience taught us the importance of clear communication, delegation of tasks, and maintaining a calm, focused approach under pressure. Through this project, I honed my skills in project management, technical design, and interdisciplinary cooperation. I gained a deeper understanding of space travel logistics, life support systems, and the psychological, social, and ethical considerations of long-duration missions.
